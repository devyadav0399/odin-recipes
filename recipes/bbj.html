Bread Butter Jam

Bread Butter Jam Recipe is the simplest and the basic sandwich recipe even a kid can make it easily. Usually kids make this for fireless cooking in school competitions.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Jam

Steps

  1. Take 3 fresh bread slices
  2. Apply butter on one slice and jam on another slice
  3. Take the third slice and apply jam on it again
  4. Cover it and make a sandwich
  5. Serve it as it is or toast it
