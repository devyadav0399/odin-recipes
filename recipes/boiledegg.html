Boiled Egg

Boiled eggs are eggs, typically from a chicken, cooked with their shells unbroken, usually by immersion in boiling water.

Ingredients

  • Boiled Egg
  • Water
  • Pinch of Salt (optional)

Steps

  1. Place eggs in the bottom of a saucepan
  2. Fill the saucepan with water, submerging the eggs
  3. Boil for 10 mintues on medium-high heat
  4. Place eggs in cold or running water
  5. Peel the eggshells and serve
