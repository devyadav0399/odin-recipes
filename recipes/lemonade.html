Lemonade

Lemonade is a sweetened lemon-flavored beverage. There are varieties of lemonade found throughout the world. In North America and South Asia, cloudy lemonade is the most common variety.

There it is traditionally a homemade drink using lemon juice, water, and a sweetener such as cane sugar, simple syrup or honey.

Ingredients

  • Lemon
  • Sugar
  • Water

Steps

  1. Add sugar to the water to make a sugar syrup
  2. Juice the lemons
  3. Pour the lemon juice and sugar syrup into a container
  4. Refrigerate for 30 mins and serve with ice
